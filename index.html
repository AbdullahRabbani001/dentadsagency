Who pays for the ad spend?

+

The ad spend is paid directly by you to Google and Facebook, giving you full transparency and control over your budget. We charge a separate monthly management fee for strategy, setup, optimization, and ongoing campaign management.

What is the minimum ad budget required?

+

We recommend a minimum monthly ad budget of $1,500‚Äì$2,000 to generate consistent and measurable results. That said, the exact budget can be adjusted based on your practice size, location, competition, and growth goals.

What does your management fee include?

+

Our management fee covers:

  • Campaign strategy & planning
  • Google & Facebook ad setup
  • Audience targeting & keyword research
  • Conversion & call tracking setup
  • Ongoing optimization & scaling
  • Performance monitoring & reporting

  • Preferred booking method (calls, forms, or calendar)
